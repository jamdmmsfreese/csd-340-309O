My Story

Chapter 1: About Me

I grew up in the city. I now live in a suburban area. The city has grown a lot in the past several years.

I went to small private schools growing up. After high school, I went to work before trying college. I finally started college 5-years after high school. I finally graduated six years later!

As a child, I enjoyed dancing and running. In high school, I was on the track team. I was also a cheerleader.

I grew up in a house with two siblings and my parents. I have many other relatives but they do not live close by. There are a few that I now pretty well, some a know a little bit, and many I have never met.
